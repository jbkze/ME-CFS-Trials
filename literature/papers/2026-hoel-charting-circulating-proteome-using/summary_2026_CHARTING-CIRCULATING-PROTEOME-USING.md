## Summary

Measured thousands of proteins in patients' blood and found many that differ from healthy people — signs of an overactive immune system and poor blood flow to the muscles.

## Why this matters

The largest blood-protein study in ME/CFS so far; it maps what is going wrong in the body and could lead to a blood test and new treatment targets.
