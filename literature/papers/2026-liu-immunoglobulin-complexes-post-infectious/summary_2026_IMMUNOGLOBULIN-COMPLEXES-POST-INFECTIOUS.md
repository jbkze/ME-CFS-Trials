## Summary

Antibodies (immune proteins called IgG) taken from ME/CFS patients' blood were shown to shatter the energy-producing structures inside blood-vessel cells and change the cells' inflammatory signals.

_(Full text in `paper.txt`; the summary above is the curated plain-language abstract — deepen from the full text when reading.)_

## Why this matters

From Scheibenbogen and Prusty: directly demonstrates that ME/CFS antibodies damage cellular energy machinery — supporting antibody-removing treatments such as immunoadsorption and highlighting why patients' blood vessels may not function normally.
