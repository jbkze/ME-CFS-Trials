## Summary

Antibodies produced during COVID infection persist in long COVID patients and, when transferred into mice, caused fatigue, coordination problems, and nerve damage — proving these 'self-attacking' antibodies directly cause neurological symptoms.

## Why this matters

High-impact proof (in Cell, one of science's top journals) that autoantibodies physically cause post-viral illness — directly supports the antibody-removing treatments being developed for ME/CFS.
