## Summary

Tracking both post-COVID and ME/CFS patients over nearly three years showed their nerve, blood-pressure regulation, and thinking-speed changes were largely the same — and that post-COVID patients increasingly resembled established ME/CFS over time.

_(Full text in `paper.txt`; the summary above is the curated plain-language abstract — deepen from the full text when reading.)_

## Why this matters

Provides direct longitudinal evidence that post-COVID and ME/CFS share the same underlying disease process, strengthening the case for treatments that would work for both conditions.
