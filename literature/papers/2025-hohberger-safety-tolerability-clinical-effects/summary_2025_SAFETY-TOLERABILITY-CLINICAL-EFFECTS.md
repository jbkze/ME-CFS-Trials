## Summary

In a rigorous trial with a placebo group, a drug that neutralises harmful 'self-attacking' antibodies reduced fatigue and improved quality of life in Long COVID patients.

## Why this matters

The first properly controlled trial showing benefit from removing these antibodies — strong support for antibody-targeting treatments that are also being explored in ME/CFS.
