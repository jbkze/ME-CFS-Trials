## Summary

Over 1,000 adults with ME/CFS filled out a detailed symptom questionnaire; on average their overall symptom burden was severe, with cognitive problems (like brain fog) rated the worst and fatigue the most universal symptom, and women reporting a heavier symptom load than men.

_(Full text in `paper.txt`; the summary above is the curated plain-language abstract — deepen from the full text when reading.)_

## Why this matters

One of the largest patient-reported symptom-burden surveys in ME/CFS to date; mainly descriptive rather than mechanistic, but useful for quantifying how disabling the illness is across body systems and for designing outcome measures for future drug trials.
