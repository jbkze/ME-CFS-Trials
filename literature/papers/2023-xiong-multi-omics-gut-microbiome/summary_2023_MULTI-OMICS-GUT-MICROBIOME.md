## Summary

A detailed gut analysis showed ME/CFS patients lack bacteria that make butyrate (a fuel that nourishes the gut lining); the pattern differed depending on how long they had been ill.

## Why this matters

One of the most rigorous microbiome studies in ME/CFS, strengthening the gut-disease link and possible treatments.
