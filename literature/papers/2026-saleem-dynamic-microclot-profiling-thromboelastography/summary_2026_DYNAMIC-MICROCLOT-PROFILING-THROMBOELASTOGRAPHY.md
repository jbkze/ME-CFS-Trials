# Dynamic microclot profiling: thromboelastography advances precision management in long COVID and myalgic encephalomyelitis/chronic fatigue syndrome

## Related files

## Summary

Used a specialised clotting test to measure tiny blood clots (microclots) in long COVID and ME/CFS patients, finding different clotting patterns among individuals that could guide personalised treatment.

> _No PDF available_ — Wolters Kluwer — paywalled. Retrieve via institutional access (Shibboleth) or interlibrary loan; DOI kept in `pdf:`/`url:`.

## Why this matters

Adds to the growing evidence that abnormal clotting and poor circulation contribute to ME/CFS; the profiling method it describes could help identify which patients might benefit from anti-clotting therapies.
