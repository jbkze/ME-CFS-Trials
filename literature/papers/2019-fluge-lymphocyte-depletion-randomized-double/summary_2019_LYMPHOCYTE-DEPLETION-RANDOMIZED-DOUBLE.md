# B-Lymphocyte Depletion in Patients With Myalgic Encephalomyelitis/Chronic Fatigue Syndrome: A Randomized, Double-Blind, Placebo-Controlled Trial (RituxME)

## Related files
- [[literature/papers/2019-fluge-lymphocyte-depletion-randomized-double/citation.bib]]

## Summary

A large, rigorous trial of rituximab — a drug that wipes out B-cells (an immune cell type) — found it did NOT help ME/CFS patients overall.

## Why this matters

A pivotal negative result that ended hopes for rituximab and redirected the field; a landmark in ME/CFS research history. Bergen (Fluge/Mella) group.
