## Summary

Confirmed, across several laboratories, that a tiny 'gate' which lets calcium into certain immune cells doesn't work properly in ME/CFS patients.

_(Full text in `paper.txt`; the summary above is the curated plain-language abstract — deepen from the full text when reading.)_

## Why this matters

Because independent labs got the same result, this faulty cell 'gate' is one of the most reliable biological signs of ME/CFS and a possible drug target.
