## Summary

Using an AI method that looks at combinations of gene variants (not just single genes), researchers re-analysed the large DecodeME genetic dataset and found over 22,000 reproducible variant-combinations pointing to about 250 'core' genes involved in nerve/brain signalling, inflammation, cell-stress response, and calcium handling; 76 of these genes are also linked to long COVID.

## Why this matters

A deeper follow-up analysis of the DecodeME genetics study already in this database — it turns raw genetic data into specific biological pathways and proposes two existing drugs (ampligen and apremilast) as candidates worth testing.
