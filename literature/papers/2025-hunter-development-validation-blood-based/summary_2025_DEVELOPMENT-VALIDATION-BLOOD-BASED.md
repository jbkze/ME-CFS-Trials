## Summary

Developed and validated a blood test using a novel technique that reads three-dimensional patterns of DNA folding; the test correctly identified ME/CFS patients versus healthy people with 96% accuracy in an independent test group.

_(Full text in `paper.txt`; the summary above is the curated plain-language abstract — deepen from the full text when reading.)_

## Why this matters

One of the highest-accuracy diagnostic blood tests ever reported for ME/CFS — a disease that currently has no objective diagnostic test — and reveals specific immune and brain-inflammation pathways as potential drug targets.
