## Summary

Found that ME/CFS patients' blood chemistry shows a widespread 'slowed-down' metabolism — the body acting as if in a low-energy survival mode.

## Why this matters

A widely-cited study arguing ME/CFS is a measurable disorder of how the body makes and uses energy, not a psychological problem.
