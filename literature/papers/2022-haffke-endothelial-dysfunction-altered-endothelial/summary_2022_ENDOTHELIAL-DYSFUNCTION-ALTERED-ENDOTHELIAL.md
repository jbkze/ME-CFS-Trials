## Summary

Found that the inner lining of blood vessels (the endothelium) doesn't work properly in many ME/CFS and Long COVID patients.

## Why this matters

Supports the idea that poor blood-vessel function drives ME/CFS symptoms, and points to treatments that target the vessels. From the Scheibenbogen group.
