## Summary

Using exercise tests with catheters in the blood vessels, found that ME/CFS patients' blood doesn't return and circulate properly during exertion ('preload failure'), alongside nerve abnormalities.

## Why this matters

Gives a physical, measurable explanation for exercise intolerance and points to circulation- and nerve-targeted treatments. Harvard (Systrom) group.
