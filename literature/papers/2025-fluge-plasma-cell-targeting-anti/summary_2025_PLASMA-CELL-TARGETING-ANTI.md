## Summary

A small trial gave 10 patients daratumumab — a drug that clears the immune cells which make antibodies; 6 of 10 improved and several stayed better for one to two years.

_(Full text in `paper.txt`; the summary above is the curated plain-language abstract — deepen from the full text when reading.)_

## Why this matters

The first trial of this approach in ME/CFS; the strong, lasting improvement supports the idea that misguided antibodies drive the illness, and a larger trial is planned.
