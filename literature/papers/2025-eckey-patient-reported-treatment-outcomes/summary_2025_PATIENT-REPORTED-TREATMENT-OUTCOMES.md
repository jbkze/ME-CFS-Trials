## Summary

A survey of nearly 4,000 patients across ME/CFS and long COVID asked what helped them from over 150 treatments; people with both conditions reported remarkably similar responses — and graded exercise therapy was rated least helpful.

_(Full text in `paper.txt`; the summary above is the curated plain-language abstract — deepen from the full text when reading.)_

## Why this matters

The largest real-world treatment survey in ME/CFS/long COVID, showing the two conditions behave similarly and pointing to which symptom subtypes (e.g. autonomic, cognitive) respond best to which drug classes — directly useful for designing future trials.
