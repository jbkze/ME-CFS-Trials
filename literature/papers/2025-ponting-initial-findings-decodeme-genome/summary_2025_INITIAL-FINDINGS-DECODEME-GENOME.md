## Summary

The largest genetic study of ME/CFS (over 15,000 patients) found eight spots in our DNA linked to the illness, several involved in how the body fights infection.

_(Full text in `paper.txt`; the summary above is the curated plain-language abstract — deepen from the full text when reading.)_

## Why this matters

First strong genetic evidence that ME/CFS has a biological basis involving the immune and nervous systems — and a source of new drug targets.
