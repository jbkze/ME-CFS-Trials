## Summary

ME/CFS patients who received hyperbaric oxygen treatment (breathing pure oxygen in a pressurised chamber) showed improved symptoms and physical function; brain scans also revealed changes in an area linked to fatigue.

_(Full text in `paper.txt`; the summary above is the curated plain-language abstract — deepen from the full text when reading.)_

## Why this matters

Involves Carmen Scheibenbogen's team and is one of the first studies to link symptom improvement with measurable brain changes after oxygen treatment — potentially pointing to both a therapy and a biological marker of response.
