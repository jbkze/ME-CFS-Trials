## Summary

Examined proteins in the fluid that bathes the brain and spinal cord, and found inflammation-related patterns that matched how severe patients were and whether they also had a racing heart on standing up (a condition called POTS).

_(Full text in `paper.txt`; the summary above is the curated plain-language abstract — deepen from the full text when reading.)_

## Why this matters

One of the first studies of this fluid in ME/CFS; the patterns it found could become markers for sorting patients into the right treatment groups.
