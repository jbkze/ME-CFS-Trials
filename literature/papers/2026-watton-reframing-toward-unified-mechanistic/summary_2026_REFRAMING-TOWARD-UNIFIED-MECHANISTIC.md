## Summary

A detailed review tracing how ME/CFS research shifted from uncertainty to a clear biological picture: advances in long COVID research revealed multiple converging faults — immune disruption, energy-factory failure in cells, damaged blood vessels, abnormal blood clotting — which often only become visible when the body is put under physical stress.

## Why this matters

From Prusty (whose lab showed that ME/CFS patients' antibodies can shatter cell energy machinery): this unified framework pulls together all major biological findings and argues that stress-revealed pathology should drive how trials are designed and which patients are selected — potentially making future treatments much more targeted.
