## Summary

Showed that the worse a patient's gut symptoms are, the worse their main ME/CFS symptoms and body-wide inflammation tend to be — suggesting the gut is part of the disease, not a side issue.

## Why this matters

Supports treating gut problems as central to ME/CFS, which could shape who is enrolled in drug trials and how improvement is measured.
