## Summary

Pulls together evidence that ME/CFS muscles get too little blood and build up salt (sodium) and calcium, which damages the muscle cells' energy units.

## Why this matters

A cornerstone of the Wirth/Scheibenbogen theory and the direct rationale for their drug MDC002, which aims to fix exactly this muscle problem.
