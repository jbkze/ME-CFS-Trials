## Summary

Directly compared the gut bacteria of ME/CFS patients and long COVID patients, finding shared changes — particularly missing 'good' bacteria and excess of others — that were not seen in healthy people.

_(Full text in `paper.txt`; the summary above is the curated plain-language abstract — deepen from the full text when reading.)_

## Why this matters

The first head-to-head comparison of gut microbiome changes in ME/CFS and long COVID; the overlap supports the idea that both conditions share underlying gut-related biology, pointing toward shared treatment strategies such as gut microbiome repair.
