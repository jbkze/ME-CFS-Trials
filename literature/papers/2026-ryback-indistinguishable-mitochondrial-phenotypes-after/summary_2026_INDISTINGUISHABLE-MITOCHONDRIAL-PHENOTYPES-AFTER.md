## Summary

A large, carefully pre-planned study tried to repeat a well-known 2016 finding that blood serum from ME/CFS patients changes how healthy muscle cells produce energy. Using blood from 67 patients and 53 healthy people and over 1,700 lab measurements, they found no such difference this time.

## Why this matters

A rigorous negative replication from Chris Ponting's group (who also leads the DecodeME genetics study already in this database) — it challenges a widely-cited piece of evidence for a 'blood-borne factor' in ME/CFS and shows the field is actively re-testing its own key claims.
