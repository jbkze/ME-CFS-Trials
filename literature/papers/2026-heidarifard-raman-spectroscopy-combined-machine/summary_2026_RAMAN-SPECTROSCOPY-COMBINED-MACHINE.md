## Summary

A light-based test (Raman spectroscopy) combined with AI correctly distinguished ME patients from healthy people about 80–85% of the time using chemical changes in blood plasma, both at rest and after a standardised stress test.

_(Full text in `paper.txt`; the summary above is the curated plain-language abstract — deepen from the full text when reading.)_

## Why this matters

Demonstrates a rapid, potentially low-cost screening tool for a disease that currently has no objective diagnostic test, and shows the test still works after exertion — relevant for detecting patients whose symptoms worsen with activity.
